Pumpkin Spice Latte

okay, so, what is this website?

as you already know, psl's are the most delicious fall beverages known to mankind, but they've inspired something inside of us to create a programming language based on the concept behind a euphoria-inducing fall dream. this website acts as a portal to learn Pumpkin Spice Latte, the programming language. not only will you learn the language, you'll learn some of the basic concepts behind a nerdy field called computer science.

with the growing demand for girls in computer science, we deemed that it was necessary to create a language that basics could relate to. we've adopted the common language of girls to a programming language that gives them an easy transition into the world of coding. our goal is to bring in girls that would not necessarily consider computer science as a field of interest, and, hopefully, increase the female presence in this field.
